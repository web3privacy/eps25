Ethereum Privacy Stack

Diving into the culture of the Ethereum privacy

21 Nov 2025 AT La Rural
Tickets

If privacy is a human right, then we need to protect it.

A global privacy summit at Devconnect bringing together Ethereum builders, protocol maintainers, and advocates. Featuring Vitalik Buterin, Roger Dingledine, Ameen Soleimani, and 20+ speakers celebrating privacy acceleration.

Join us Nov 21, 2025 in Buenos Aires at Devconnect.

FAQ
What is Web3Privacy Now?

A collective building next-generation privacy by connecting advocates, tracking 700 projects, and hosting events for 8,000+ attendees.

Who is the event for?

People interested in privacy, freedom, human rights in Ethereum ecosystem. We aim to make our content accessible to everyone. As a part of "Stack" - discuss privacy topics in their broader context.

How do I get to the venue?

The venue is La Rural - located in the center of Buenos Aires in front of the metro station Plaza Italia of line D. The address is Av. Sarmiento 2704 (Google Maps).

I have another question, where can I contact you?
