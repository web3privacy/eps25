FAQ
What is Web3Privacy Now?

Web3privacy now Collective building a future foundation of the next-generation of privacy by fostering collaboration among privacy advocates from Ethereum to Tor, maintaining Privacy dashboard with 700 projects, facilitating privacy & freedom focused events visited by 8 000 people.

Who is the event for?

People interested in privacy, freedom, human rights in Ethereum ecosystem. We aim to make our content accessible to everyone. As a part of "Stack" - discuss privacy topics in their broader context.

How do I get to the venue?

The venue is La Rural - located in the center of Buenos Aires in front of the metro station Plaza Italia of line D. The address is Av. Sarmiento 2704 (Google Maps).

I have another question, where can I contact you?

You can contact the organizing team or the community around the event in our Telegram Community
